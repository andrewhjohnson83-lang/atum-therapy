Our Therapeutic Offerings

Each session draws from a curated suite of healing modalities, selected and woven together by David Landcaster to address your unique needs — body, mind, and spirit.

Infra-red Crystal Bio Mat

Delve into deep relaxation as the Infra-red Crystal Bio Mat uses gentle heat and the natural healing power of crystals to soothe your muscles, ease joint pain, and improve circulation.

Orbital Body Buffer

Rejuvenate your body with the rhythmic, circular motions of the 15–5 lb Orbital Body Buffer, easing muscle stiffness and accelerating recovery while invigorating energy flow.

Acupressure

Acupressure taps into your body's natural healing abilities, using precise pressure points to release tension, promote circulation, and enhance your body's life force energy for holistic well-being.

Myofascial Release

Experience profound release from chronic pain and stiffness as Myofascial Release gently smooths the fascia, restoring flexibility and initiating deep-seated healing in connective tissues.

Neuromuscular Therapy

Targeting the root of muscle pain, Neuromuscular Therapy applies meticulous pressure and friction to eliminate spots of intense body tension and restore balance to the nervous system.

Shiatsu Therapy

Shiatsu Therapy blends therapeutic pressure with an ancient, meditative touch to harmonize your body's energy pathways, offering a pathway to both physical and emotional equilibrium.

Thai Massage

Embrace a dynamic blend of stretching and pressure with Thai Massage, a dance-like therapy that energizes and balances the body, enhancing flexibility and peaceful mind-body synergy.

MPS Dolphin Micro-Stimulator

A revolutionary device offering non-invasive acupuncture-like stimulation, aiding in pain relief and promoting the body's innate healing responses.

Sound Healing Music

Immersive sound frequencies that support relaxation, emotional release, and restoration of the body's natural rhythms throughout each session.

Resolution Therapy

Mental and emotional reframing sessions that help clients recognize and shift repeating life patterns, unhelpful thought habits, and deeply held emotional imprints.

Spiritual Counseling

Drawing on two decades of study and lived experience, David provides spiritual guidance to support clarity, purpose, and connection throughout your healing journey.

Energy Work

Through breath awareness and intuitive listening, David works to help release tension, restore balance, calm the mind, and support the body's natural ability to heal and regulate itself.
